04

Limitations
& alternatives.

An honest look at what's hard — and the five paths we ruled out.

Real limitationsThree constraints we plan around

Agentic AI has real limits
we plan around.

Agentic AI isn't a chatbot. It's a system that plans, reasons, retrieves, and executes through tools. That agency is where the value comes from — and where the limits get severe.

01
Operational reliability
Performance degrades on long tasks
Reliability falls when choosing among many tools or in ambiguous contexts
Error recovery and contradiction handling remain fragile
Stochastic outputs are harder to audit — a constraint for processes that need repeatability
02
Cybersecurity
Greater autonomy = broader attack surface, higher-impact failures
Prompt injection can manipulate tool use and sabotage software
Remote code execution, sequential tool attacks, memory poisoning, supply-chain risks
Persistent-state corruption can sharply increase attack success rates
03
Data storage & retrieval
Performance depends heavily on the quality and organization of internal data
Fragmented or siloed information reduces output quality
Inconsistent labeling and ambiguous access rights block retrieval
Without strong data infrastructure, agents generate noise rather than value
