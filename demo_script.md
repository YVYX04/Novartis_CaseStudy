# NovaSphere — 3-Minute Demo Script
**Presenter:** Yvan Richard · HSG Strategy Communication · Spring 2026
**File:** `index.html` — open directly in a browser (no server needed)
**Total time:** ~3 minutes

---

## Pre-demo setup
- `index.html` open in browser, full screen
- Welcome screen showing (sphere animation visible)
- Font bumped for visibility if presenting on projector

---

## [0:00 – 0:18] The Welcome

> *Sphere animation. "Good morning, Mary." glows on screen. Loader bar fills. Text reads: "Your agents are ready. Let's make today count."*

**SPEAK:**
"This is NovaSphere — a personalized AI command center built for Novartis commercial teams across the Western European Cluster. It opens knowing who you are, where you are in the day, and what's already happened overnight. Let's step in."

`[ACTION: Click "Skip →"]`

---

## [0:18 – 0:40] The Dashboard

> *Dashboard fades in. Five agent cards. Stat strip. Cluster pulse map with live country dots.*

**SPEAK:**
"Mary's dashboard is live. Five AI agents — each tuned to a specific role and moment in the workday. And a live cluster pulse across all seven WEC countries. We're going to walk through three of these agents right now."

`[ACTION: Click the "Morning Briefing" agent card]`

---

## [0:40 – 1:25] Agent 1 — Morning Briefing

> *Agent view opens. Pre-built output visible: headline "ACT BEFORE 10:00", three signal rows, cluster stats, three recommended next actions.*

**SPEAK:**
"It's 7 AM. Before Mary's opened a single email, NovaSphere has already synthesized her overnight inbox, cluster KPIs, and Rx trends into one briefing.

The top signal: Belgium's Cosentyx campaign dropped to 18% open rate against a 28% target. Action required before the 10:00 team meeting.

Below that — Marc Huber needs an HCP data pack for Dr. Keller by 13:00. The Pre-Call Intel agent already has it staged.

And the win: the Nordics webinar is at 125% of registration target — four high-value cardiologists confirmed from Oslo and Stockholm.

Three recommended next actions — each one clickable, each tied to a specific agent. This isn't a summary. It's a decision engine."

`[ACTION: Click the back arrow or "Today" in the nav to return]`

---

## [1:25 – 1:35] Pivot — Inbox

`[ACTION: Click "Inbox" in the top nav]`

**SPEAK:**
"Every screen in NovaSphere has the AI layer built in. Look at Sophie's email — the Belgium alert."

**SPEAK:**
"The AI reads the email and immediately suggests the next action — with the right agent and a time estimate. The inbox isn't just a reading view. It's a trigger surface."
