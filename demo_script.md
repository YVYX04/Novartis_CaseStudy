## Pre-demo setup
- `index.html` open in browser, full screen
- Welcome screen showing (sphere animation visible)
- Font bumped for visibility if presenting on projector

## The Welcome

"Imagine your Monday no longer feels like a Monday.

You already know what happened over the weekend, you are prepared for every meeting, and your schedule is no longer disorganized chaos.

Meet NovaSphere: the agentic AI platform that makes this possible."

---

## The Dashboard

"Mary has just started using NovaSphere, and already her day feels different. From the homepage, she can see what needs her attention: emails, deadlines, and the live progress of her marketing campaign. And when it is time to act, she does not start from scratch. She calls on her personal team of AI agents."

---

## Agent 1 — Morning Briefing

"Mary starts with one of her most useful agents: the Morning Briefing. Before she has opened a single email, NovaSphere has already turned her overnight inbox, cluster KPIs, and prescription trends into a clear briefing.

The key signal is immediate: Belgium's Cosentyx campaign is underperforming, with an 18% open rate against a 28% target. From there, Mary can review the full context and see which actions deserve priority."

## Multiple Agents Presentation

"With NovaSphere, Mary’s agents are always one step ahead.
Need to check her calendar? Handled.

She just remembered a call with Dr. Keller from Bern, no worries. The Pre-Call Intel agent has already gathered the context, insights, and talking points she needs.

## Customization w/ John

Here is John’s NovaSphere. John is not Mary. He is more junior, his responsibilities are different, and his day does not look the same.

So his NovaSphere should not look the same either.

For example, John has the Skill Sprint Agent. At the end of the day, he can use it to quickly review the concepts, products, and insights he encountered during work. Instead of losing that knowledge, NovaSphere helps him turn it into progress.

## Conclusion

Novasphere is tomorrow's solution built today; just feel it.
